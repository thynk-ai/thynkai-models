# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant.

## Pledge

We commit to a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

We expect respectful, constructive communication and good-faith technical disagreement.
We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, or repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Report issues privately to maintainers. Maintainers may warn or ban contributors when necessary.
